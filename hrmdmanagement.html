Company Overview

HRMD Management is a consulting group dedicated to help our clients meet their goals. HRMD brings the right tools for each client, to align them with the business model that best suits their long term goals.

Insurance Specialists provide accurate and timely verification of patient insurance and appropriate compliance with insurance, provider, and facility, ensuring that the patient insurance information is up to date and coverage benefits are clear before accessing treatment. Also, we complete, timely and accurate identification and submission of prior authorization requests to the insurance, interact with insurance agents, medical staff, and other departments as necessary to request documentation pertaining to the process, and advise Providers of procedures that can and cannot be performed in compliance with insurance standards and protocols.

  • Verifications (Benefits & Coverage)
  • PreAuthorizations (All types of services)
  • Referrals: PCP or Insurance (OV)
  • LOP - Auto claims & WC
  • Financials: Fees

Billing Specialists will be responsible for reading and understanding the Provider's Progress Notes and Medical Terminology to be able to create Claims, determine what needs to be charged/billed to the insurance and guarantee timely filing, alert the Insurance Department of inconsistencies in diagnosis or billing codes to prevent denials from insurances.
