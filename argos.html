Company Overview

Cementos Argos is a growing multinational that consolidates its presence in sixteen countries and territories with emerging and developed economies. For over eighty years, it has been building a history and today stands as the number one cement and concrete company in Colombia and one of the most relevant in the United States, the Caribbean, and Central America. It has nearly 7,000 employees across its three regions, 11 cement plants, 197 concrete plants, 7 aggregate plants, 9 clinker mills, 29 ports and terminals, almost 1,700 mixer trucks, over 1,500 train wagons, and 4 owned ships. Its total installed capacity is 24 million tons of cement. In 2022, it exported to 25 destinations.
