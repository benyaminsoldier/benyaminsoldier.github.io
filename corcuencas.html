Company Overview

The corporate purpose of CORCUENCAS, according to its bylaws, is to "serve as an instrument for the promotion, planning, participation, promotion, financing, execution of policies, plans, programs, projects, works, and/or actions for the protection of natural resources, the environment, watersheds, and in general, anything that benefits these, framed within sustainable social economic development and peaceful coexistence, throughout the national territory, especially in the department of Tolima."

Within this framework, the main objective of the Corporation is to serve as an instrument and/or mechanism of support, fostering close collaboration between the Public and Private sectors responsible for or involved in conservation, protection, and management of natural resources, the environment, watersheds, and social construction.
