Company Overview

In Fabricación y Lavandería Textil S.A.S - FALATEX, we provide a comprehensive package of services for the creation and production of textiles, including design, product development, cutting, sewing, outsourcing, embroidery, laundry, finishing, and packaging.With approximately 100 direct collaborators, we have established ourselves as one of the largest laundries in the northeastern region of Colombia.

We are a textile group that sets trends, inspires, and satisfies the fashion needs of the market through a strategic synergy among clients, collaborators, and shareholders.Our aim is to influence global fashion by being leaders in the production of textile garments and their commercialization through digital platforms.

Since the inception of this ambitious project, we have outlined sustainable, profitable, and transparent business growth as a strategic objective.
